环境

  • JDK1.8
  • Centos7

主机-两台

  • centos7_1 :172.16.54.130
  • centos7_2 :172.16.54.128

软件资源

  • JDK1.8 :https://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/jdk8-downloads-2133151.html
